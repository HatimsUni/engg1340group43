2-6 players.
Cards rank 2-10, J/Q/K = 10, A = 1 or 11.
Each player gets 2 cards.
Score of 21 is a win, else hit or stand.
Hit: draw another card.
Over 21 is a loss, 21 is a win, lower score continues.
Stand: final score.
Game ends when everyone loses, wins, or stands.
Winners have highest scores.
Winners gain points equal to number of players in game.
