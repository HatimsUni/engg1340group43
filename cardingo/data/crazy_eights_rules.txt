Match rank or suit to discard cards.
Objective: Be the first to discard all cards.
Start with 7 cards each.
Draw pile and discard pile on table.
Play card matching suit or rank of top discard card.
Draw until a playable card is found.
"Eight" card lets player choose next suit.
First to discard all cards wins.
If draw pile is empty, game ends in a tie.
Have Fun!
