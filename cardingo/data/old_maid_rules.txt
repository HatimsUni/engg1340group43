Remove all pairs without the queen of spades.
Pair: same value and color (clubs/spades, diamonds/hearts).
Each player draws 26 cards at start.
Player having Queen of Clubs, discards it.
Opponent starts first.
Take a card from opponent, discard pairs.
Alternate turns until one player has no cards.
Loser: player with queen of spades.
Winner: player with no cards left.
Have Fun!
