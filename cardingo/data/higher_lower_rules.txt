Objective: Guess 10 cards correctly with least amount of lives lost.
Player gets 5 lives.
Draw a card from deck.
Guess if the current card is either similar, higher, or lower than the next unknown card.
Input options: "H", "L", "S"
Lose one life per one wrong guess.
Guess until player guessed right 10 times or lost all 5 lives.
Winning condition: Have remaining lives.
> 5 lives left = 5 points scored
> 4 lives left = 4 points scored
> 3 lives left = 3 points scored
> 2 lives left = 2 points scored
> 1 life left = 1 point scored
Losing condition: Have 0 life.
> 0 life left = 0 point scored
Have Fun!
